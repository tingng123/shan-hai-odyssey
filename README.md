# Shan Hai Odyssey

A 2D top-down action RPG inspired by **Black Myth: Wukong** — built in Godot 4.3+.

Explore the living pages of the ancient Chinese classic *Shan Hai Jing (Classic of Mountains and Seas)*, fight legendary corrupted bosses, and absorb their powers.

## Vision

Fast, weighty hack-and-slash combat with combo chains, perfect parries, dodge rolls, and a transformation system — all wrapped in pixel-art with Chinese ink-wash aesthetics.

## Tech Stack

- **Engine:** Godot 4.3+
- **Language:** GDScript
- **Art:** Pixel art (32×32 base, 4× upscale) + ink-wash shader effects
- **Audio:** Traditional Chinese instruments + cinematic orchestral

## Player Mechanics

- Light combos + heavy charged attacks (stamina system)
- Perfect dodge roll + perfect parry (slow-mo counter window)
- Qi meter for special skills
- 4 unlockable combat stances
- Transformation system — become a mini-version of defeated bosses
