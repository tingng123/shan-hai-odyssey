**GAME DEVELOPMENT PROMPT – “Shan Hai Odyssey”**

You are an expert 2D game designer and senior Godot 4.3+ developer. Build a complete game development plan + starter project structure for a **2D top-down action RPG** called **Shan Hai Odyssey**.

**Core Vision**  
A love letter to Black Myth: Wukong’s intense boss fights and mythic storytelling, but in beautiful 2D top-down style. The player explores the living pages of the ancient Chinese classic **Shan Hai Jing (Classic of Mountains and Seas)**, fighting one legendary boss per chapter. Each boss is a faithful yet epic interpretation of a real creature from the book. Think Hades-style fluid combat meets the soul-like tension and Chinese ink-painting aesthetics of Black Myth: Wukong.

**Genre & Gameplay Style**  
- 2D top-down action RPG (WASD + mouse or controller twin-stick)  
- Fast, weighty hack-and-slash combat with combo chains, charged heavies, perfect parries, and dodge rolls  
- Chapter-based semi-open exploration (not full open world)  
- Light RPG progression: collect Ling Essence, ancient relics, lore scrolls, skill tree  
- Boss fights are the highlight — multi-phase, spectacle-driven, with unique mechanics that require learning patterns (exactly like Black Myth: Wukong bosses)  

**Story Summary**  
You play as **Ling Yun**, a young guardian who discovers a forbidden copy of the Shan Hai Jing. The book pulls you inside its mythical world. Ancient beasts have awakened and corrupted the lands. Your quest: defeat the corrupted chapter bosses, restore harmony, and uncover the hidden truth behind the ancient myths before the realms collapse into chaos.

**Core Loop**  
Explore → Fight regular yaoguai enemies → Collect Ling Essence & relics → Reach epic boss arena → Spectacular multi-phase boss fight → Absorb boss power (new ability/transformation) → Upgrade at the central Temple hub → Choose next chapter.

**Player Mechanics (Black Myth: Wukong inspired but 2D)**  
- Light attack combos + Heavy charged attacks (stamina system)  
- Perfect dodge roll + Perfect Parry window (creates slow-mo counter opportunity)  
- Qi meter for special skills  
- 4 unlockable Stances that change combat feel  
- Transformation system: temporarily turn into a mini-version of defeated bosses for new movement/attacks (e.g. Bi Fang fire dash, Taotie devour projectile)  
- Health gourd that refills at shrines (Black Myth style)

**Art & Audio Direction**  
- Pixel-art style (32×32 base, 4× upscale) mixed with traditional Chinese ink-wash brush effects, dynamic god rays, and floating qi particles  
- Color palette: misty greens, blood reds, golden ancient tones  
- Boss designs: massive, screen-filling, terrifying yet majestic — stay loyal to Shan Hai Jing descriptions  
- Music: live guzheng + dizi + epic orchestral fusion (traditional Chinese instruments + cinematic swells)  
- Sound design: heavy impact hits, roaring beasts, echoing ancient chants

**Chapters & Bosses (6 main chapters + final boss)**  
Each chapter is one self-contained realm with exploration, side paths, minor enemies, and one giant boss at the end.

1. **Chapter 1 – Flaming Peaks**  
   Boss: **Bi Fang** (one-legged fire bird) – aerial dives, fire rings, omen flames that predict attacks.

2. **Chapter 2 – Gluttonous Caverns**  
   Boss: **Taotie** (demonic glutton) – vacuum suck-in, multi-mouth swallows, projectile eating phases.

3. **Chapter 3 – Chaotic Wastelands**  
   Boss: **Hundun** (primordial chaos beast) – formless shape-shifting, reality-warping AOE, unpredictable patterns.

4. **Chapter 4 – Serpent Seas**  
   Boss: **Bashe** (giant elephant-eating snake) – coiling wraps, poison pools, underground burrows.

5. **Chapter 5 – Eclipse Heavens**  
   Boss: **Tiangou** (Heavenly Dog) – darkness eclipse, moon-swallowing laser beams, shadow pack summons.

6. **Chapter 6 – Illusionary Fox Realm**  
   Boss: **Nine-Tailed Fox** – mass illusions, charm mind-control, clone swarm fights.

**Final Chapter – The Four Perils**  
Ultimate boss: **Qiongqi** (the final corrupted peril) – combines elements from all previous bosses.

**Technical Requirements (Godot 4.3+)**  
- Use TileMapLayer + Parallax for levels  
- Navigation2D / A* for enemy pathfinding  
- State machine for player & bosses  
- Shader effects for ink-wash, fire, qi glow  
- Save system, skill tree UI, boss health phases  
- Pixel-perfect camera with shake and slow-mo on parries  
- Export ready for PC + Steam Deck (later Switch/mobile stretch goals)

**Development Milestones (suggested order)**  
1. Player controller + basic combat + dodge/parry (1 week)  
2. First chapter map + Bi Fang boss prototype (2 weeks)  
3. Transformation system + 2 more bosses  
4. Full 6 chapters + final boss  
5. Polish, lore, UI, audio, balancing
